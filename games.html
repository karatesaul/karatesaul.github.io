Half/Way

Half/Way is a competitive multiplayer series of minigames in development for tablets. We started as a class project for Game Design Practicum, and continued on our own time afterwards for the purposes of submitting to IGF. Still a work in progress, we originally developed in C# XNA, but moved to Unity.

Zombeo

Zombeo is an epic battle between the zombies and the vaccinated humans for the fate of mankind! It is a social game on the Sifteo cubes made in under 48 hours for the Sifteo Game Jam at University of California: Santa Cruz. My team of 3 and I won Best Use of the Sifteo Cubes for our innovative design idea to use the cubes as ID tags for the players.
